What makes our brand special?

Decorum India Pvt Ltd. Established in 2020 , is a business that specializes in manufacturing and selling a wide range of hardware products that are commonly used in households. These products may include handles , door knobs , hinges , Cabinet Handle , Door Closer , Lock Body , Tendom other hardware items.

It is core value of Decorum India to provide high-quality products that are durable and functional, as well as meeting the needs of their customers. Company also offer warranties and after-sales support to ensure customer satisfaction.
